The Innovation Hub

Where Ideas
Become Reality

The £200M Luminaris Innovation Hub is home to 12 state-of-the-art laboratories, a biotech incubator, a design studio, a 500-seat auditorium, and co-working spaces for over 300 student entrepreneurs.

Since opening in 2024, the Hub has already supported 48 student startups, secured £12M in seed funding, and filed 23 new patents. It is the beating heart of Luminaris's innovation ecosystem.

The Process

How to
Apply

Our streamlined application process is designed to be straightforward. Follow these five steps to begin your Luminaris journey.

01
🔍

Explore Programs

Browse our 180+ programs, attend virtual open days, and speak with current students to find your perfect fit.

02
📋

Check Requirements

Review the entry requirements, language proficiency standards, and any additional prerequisites for your chosen program.

03
📝

Submit Application

Complete your online application form, upload your personal statement, academic transcripts, and letters of recommendation.

04
💬

Interview (if required)

Some programs require an interview. These can be conducted in person or virtually and are a chance for us to get to know you.

05
🎉

Receive Your Offer

Successful applicants will receive a formal offer letter. Accept your place, secure accommodation, and get ready for Luminaris!

Entry Requirements

What We
Look For

📜

Academic Results

Minimum of 3 A-levels at grades AAB or equivalent. Strong performance in subjects relevant to your chosen program.

✍️

Personal Statement

A compelling 650-word personal statement demonstrating your passion for the subject, relevant experience, and future ambitions.

📨

References

One academic reference from a teacher or school counsellor who can speak to your academic ability and potential.

🌐

English Proficiency

IELTS 6.5 overall (no less than 6.0 in any component) or TOEFL iBT 90+ for non-native English speakers.

🎓

Bachelor's Degree

A recognised bachelor's degree at 2:1 (upper second class honours) or above, or international equivalent.

💼

Work Experience

For professional programs like MBA, a minimum of 2 years relevant professional work experience is required.

📄

Statement of Purpose

A 1,000-word statement outlining your research interests, career goals, and why Luminaris is the right institution for you.

📨

Two References

Two academic or professional references who can speak to your qualifications, work ethic, and suitability for postgraduate study.

🔬

Research Proposal

A detailed 2,000-word research proposal outlining your intended area of study, methodology, and its contribution to the field.

🎓

Master's Degree

A master's degree with distinction or merit in a relevant field, or exceptional undergraduate performance considered on merit.

👩‍🏫

Supervisor Agreement

You must identify and receive informal agreement from a Luminaris faculty supervisor before submitting your application.

📰

Publications (Optional)

Any published or submitted academic work that demonstrates your research capability is a strong addition to your application.

🌍

International Qualifications

We accept qualifications from over 130 countries. Use our country-specific guide to check your equivalent entry requirements.

🗣️

English Language

IELTS 7.0 (no band below 6.5), TOEFL iBT 100+, or completion of our pre-sessional English program.

🛂

Student Visa

We provide full support for student visa applications including CAS numbers, financial guidance, and immigration advice.

💰

Financial Evidence

Evidence of sufficient funds to cover tuition and living costs for the duration of your study, as required by visa regulations.
